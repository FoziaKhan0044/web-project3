About

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    Looking for a new home? Well you have come to the right place. Our website provides the perfect platform for property sale/purchase/rent. We have the most advanced Artificial Intelligence techinques to suggest what is best for you .

  • How did it all start?

    Neighboorhood Wizard was an idea to of two undergarduates students from Muhammad Ali Jinnah University, Fozia Sohail Khan and Hammad Ben Sardar. Their goal was to give people much more insight in sale/purchase and rent of a house. Details that would let people know which areas are feasible for them to live in. 

  • Our Mission


    To empower you with the insights you need while buying/selling a property or validating its legal documents. To connect you with buyers or sellers for your property so that you may get a wide range of prices and sell/buy your property at a price you wish to

  • Why Choose Us?


    Get an edge over other websites with our online tools and a local NW Agent.
    Find homes first—you see the latest listings every 5 minutes and get new home updates 3 hours faster.
